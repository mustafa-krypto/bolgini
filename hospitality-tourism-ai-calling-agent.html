Hospitality FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can AI calling agents benefit hotels and tourism businesses?

AI calling agents help hospitality businesses by automating guest services, hotel bookings, travel coordination, and providing 24/7 multilingual support. This increases revenue by up to 35% while improving guest satisfaction and reducing operational costs.

What hotel management systems does Bolgini integrate with?

Bolgini integrates with all major Property Management Systems (PMS) including Opera Cloud, Protel Air, RMS Cloud, Hotelogix, eZee Absolute, and SkyTouch Hotel OS for seamless booking and guest management.

Can the AI handle multiple languages for international guests?

Yes, Bolgini's AI calling agent supports over 40 languages and can automatically detect guest language preferences, ensuring seamless international guest communication and enhanced guest experience.

How does AI help increase hotel revenue?

Our AI increases hotel revenue through intelligent upselling of room upgrades, spa services, dining packages, optimized booking rates, reduced no-shows, and improved guest retention. Average revenue increase is 35%.

How quickly can hotels implement the AI solution?

Implementation typically takes 1-2 weeks depending on your PMS integration requirements. We provide dedicated hospitality specialists to ensure smooth setup and staff training.
