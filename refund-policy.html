Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Bolgini (a product of KryptoMindz Technologies), we strive to provide exceptional AI calling agent services. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms for refunds, cancellations, and the refund processing timeline.

1. 14-Day Money Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money back guarantee for all new subscriptions:

  • Eligibility: Available for first-time subscribers within 14 days of initial subscription
  • Coverage: Full refund of subscription fees paid
  • Usage Limit: Guarantee applies regardless of usage within the 14-day period
  • Process: Contact our support team to initiate the refund process
  • No Questions Asked: We don't require lengthy explanations for refund requests within this period

Refund Processing Timeline

Refunds are processed within 5-7 working days and credited to your original payment method:

  • Credit Cards: 5-7 working days
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 working days
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 working days
  • International Payments: 7-10 working days

2. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your Bolgini subscription at any time:

  • Immediate Effect: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • Access Continuation: You retain access to services until the end of the paid period
  • No Cancellation Fees: We don't charge any fees for cancellation
  • Data Export: Download your data before cancellation (available for 30 days)
  • Re-activation: You can reactivate your subscription at any time

How to Cancel Your Subscription

  1. Log into your Bolgini dashboard
  2. Navigate to Account Settings > Subscription
  3. Click "Cancel Subscription" and follow the prompts
  4. Or contact our support team at support@bolgini.com

3. Refund Eligibility Conditions

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • 14-Day Guarantee: Automatic eligibility for new subscribers
  • Service Outages: Significant service downtime beyond our SLA commitments
  • Technical Issues: Unresolved technical problems affecting service functionality
  • Billing Errors: Incorrect charges or unauthorized transactions
  • Duplicate Payments: Accidental multiple payments for the same service

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Usage Fees: Call minutes already consumed beyond plan limits
  • Setup Fees: One-time setup or onboarding charges (where applicable)
  • Third-party Services: Costs for integrations with external services
  • Promotional Credits: Bonus credits or promotional offerings
  • Expired Subscriptions: Refund requests made after service termination

5. Promotional Pricing and Discounts

Special terms apply to promotional offers:

  • Launch Discount (50% OFF): Subject to 3-month minimum commitment
  • Early Cancellation: Canceling before minimum commitment may result in promotional price adjustment
  • Refund Amount: Refunds calculated based on actual amount paid, not original pricing
  • Upgrade/Downgrade: Plan changes may affect promotional pricing eligibility

6. Enterprise and Custom Plans

Custom refund terms may apply to Enterprise customers:

  • Contract Terms: Refunds governed by individual service agreements
  • Negotiated Terms: Specific refund conditions outlined in contracts
  • Annual Payments: Pro-rated refunds available for early termination
  • Custom SLAs: Service level guarantees with compensation terms

7. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact Support: Email info@bolgini.com or call +91 98730 62228
  2. Provide Details: Include account information, reason for refund, and payment details
  3. Verification: Our team will verify your account and refund eligibility
  4. Processing: Approved refunds are processed within 2 business days
  5. Confirmation: You'll receive email confirmation with refund details
  6. Credit: Refund appears in your account within 5-7 working days

8. International Customers

Additional considerations for international customers:

  • Currency Exchange: Refunds processed in original payment currency
  • Bank Fees: International transfer fees may be deducted from refund amount
  • Processing Time: Additional 2-3 days for international payments
  • Tax Refunds: Applicable taxes refunded according to local regulations

9. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision:

  • Appeal Process: Contact our management team for review
  • Documentation: Provide additional evidence supporting your request
  • Escalation: Disputes may be escalated to senior management
  • External Resolution: Legal remedies available under applicable consumer protection laws

10. Data Handling After Cancellation

Upon cancellation and refund:

  • Data Retention: Account data retained for 30 days for potential reactivation
  • Data Export: Download your data within 30 days of cancellation
  • Data Deletion: All data permanently deleted after 30 days
  • Call Recordings: Recordings deleted according to our retention policy
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data may be retained for service improvement

11. Contact for Refunds

For fastest processing of refund requests, contact us through any of these channels:

  • Email: info@bolgini.com
  • Phone Support: +91 98730 62228 (Business hours: 9 AM - 6 PM IST)
  • Live Chat: Available through your dashboard (for active subscribers)
