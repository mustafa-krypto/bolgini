How does AI calling help with insurance claims processing?

AI calling agents can handle First Notice of Loss (FNOL) calls, gather initial claim information, schedule adjuster appointments, and provide status updates to claimants. This reduces processing time by 60% and improves customer satisfaction.

Is AI calling compliant with insurance regulations?

Yes, Bolgini's AI calling agents are designed to meet strict insurance regulatory requirements including SOC 2 compliance and adherence to state insurance commission guidelines.

Can AI agents handle complex insurance inquiries?

AI agents are trained on insurance terminology, policy details, and common scenarios. They can handle 80% of routine inquiries and seamlessly transfer complex cases to human agents with full context.
