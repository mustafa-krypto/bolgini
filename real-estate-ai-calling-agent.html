Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI calling help real estate lead qualification?

AI calling agents automatically contact leads within minutes, ask qualifying questions about budget, timeline, and property preferences, then score leads based on conversion probability. This helps agents focus on high-quality prospects.

Can AI agents schedule property viewings automatically?

Yes, Bolgini AI agents can check your calendar availability, coordinate with prospects' schedules, and automatically book property viewing appointments while sending confirmations and reminders.

What information can AI agents collect about property inquiries?

AI agents can gather detailed information including budget range, preferred locations, property type, size requirements, timeline for buying/selling, financing status, and specific feature preferences.

How quickly can real estate agencies implement AI calling?

Most real estate agencies can have AI calling agents operational within 1-2 weeks. Our team handles CRM integration, lead pipeline setup, and training to ensure smooth implementation.
