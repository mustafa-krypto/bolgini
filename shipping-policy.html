Service Delivery Policy

Bolgini (a product of KryptoMindz Technologies) provides AI calling agent services delivered entirely through digital channels. As a software service provider, we don't ship physical products. Instead, we deliver access to our platform, setup, configuration, and ongoing support.

1. Digital Service Delivery Overview

Our AI calling agent service is delivered through:

  • Cloud-based Platform: Instant access to your dashboard and controls
  • API Integration: Technical documentation and integration support
  • Account Setup: Personalized configuration and onboarding
  • Training & Support: Comprehensive guides and live assistance
  • Ongoing Updates: Continuous platform improvements and new features

3. Step-by-Step Delivery Process

1

Account Creation & Payment (0-2 hours)

Upon successful payment verification, your Bolgini account is automatically created and you receive login credentials via email.

2

Welcome & Onboarding (2-24 hours)

Our team sends you welcome materials, setup guides, and schedules your personalized onboarding session.

3

Configuration & Setup (1-3 business days)

We configure your AI calling agent according to your business requirements, integrate with your systems, and set up calling campaigns.

4

Testing & Training (1-2 business days)

Comprehensive testing of your setup, team training sessions, and final optimizations before going live.

5

Go Live & Support (Same week)

Your AI calling agent goes live with full monitoring and ongoing support from our team.

Every Bolgini service delivery includes:

  • Platform Access: Immediate access to your Bolgini dashboard

  • Go-Live Support: Dedicated support during initial launch
