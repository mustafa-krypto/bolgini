How does Bolgini's AI calling agent work?

Bolgini's AI calling agent uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to conduct human-like conversations. It can handle inbound and outbound calls, provide customer support, generate leads, and analyze conversations for business insights.

What languages does Bolgini support?

Bolgini supports over 100 languages and dialects, enabling global businesses to communicate with customers in their preferred language. Our AI automatically detects and adapts to different languages during conversations.

Can Bolgini integrate with existing business systems?

Yes, Bolgini seamlessly integrates with popular CRM systems, helpdesk platforms, communication tools, and business applications through APIs and webhooks, ensuring smooth workflow integration.

Bolgini provides comprehensive analytics including call performance metrics, sentiment analysis, conversation insights, customer satisfaction scores, conversion rates, and actionable business intelligence reports.
