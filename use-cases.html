FAQ's

Common Questions About Use Cases

Which industries can benefit from Bolgini?

Bolgini works across all industries including sales, healthcare, real estate, financial services, e-commerce, education, and more. Any business that makes or receives phone calls can benefit.

How realistic are the AI conversations?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to create human-like conversations. Most recipients cannot distinguish between Bolgini and a human agent during routine interactions.

Can Bolgini handle complex customer issues?

Bolgini handles routine inquiries perfectly and uses intelligent escalation to transfer complex issues to human agents when needed, ensuring optimal customer experience.

How quickly can we implement Bolgini?

Most businesses can start using Bolgini within 24-48 hours. Our team helps with setup, training, and integration to ensure smooth implementation.

What analytics and insights does Bolgini provide?

Bolgini provides comprehensive analytics including call outcomes, sentiment analysis, conversation quality scores, lead qualification data, and actionable business insights.
