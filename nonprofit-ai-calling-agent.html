How can AI calling agents help nonprofit organizations?

AI calling agents help nonprofits by automating donor outreach, conducting fundraising calls, recruiting volunteers, promoting events, and managing donor stewardship. This increases engagement rates by up to 300% while reducing staff workload by 60%.

What nonprofit CRM systems does Bolgini integrate with?

Bolgini integrates with all major nonprofit CRM systems including Salesforce NPSP, DonorPerfect, Bloomerang, Little Green Light, NeonCRM, and MailChimp for Nonprofits.

Is AI calling compliant with nonprofit fundraising regulations?

Yes, Bolgini's AI calling agent is fully compliant with nonprofit fundraising regulations including TCPA, state solicitation laws, and Association of Fundraising Professionals (AFP) ethical guidelines.

How much can nonprofits save with AI calling automation?

Nonprofits typically save 60% of staff time while increasing donations by 45% on average. Many organizations see ROI within the first month of implementation.

Absolutely! Our AI is trained to understand donor preferences, history, and communication style to provide personalized, empathetic conversations that donors appreciate.
